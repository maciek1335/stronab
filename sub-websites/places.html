Casco Viejo in Bilbao is the historic district of the city, known for its narrow, winding streets, colorful buildings, and bars and restaurants serving pintxos. It is the heart of old Bilbao, full of landmarks such as the Basilica of Saint James and traditional shops. The Plaza Nueva, a central square, often hosts local events and markets. Casco Viejo retains the character of a typical Basque town while being one of Bilbao's main tourist attractions.

Guggenheim Museum is a world-renowned modern and contemporary art museum located in Bilbao, Spain. Designed by architect Frank Gehry, its striking, futuristic building is considered an architectural masterpiece. Opened in 1997, the museum has played a major role in transforming Bilbao into a global cultural destination.

A trip from Bilbao to Gaztelugatxe is a popular day excursion. Gaztelugatxe is a small rocky island on the Basque coast, famous for its dramatic scenery and winding stone staircase with over 200 steps leading to a tiny hermitage at the top. It's about a 45-minute drive from Bilbao and offers stunning ocean views. The site became even more famous after being featured as Dragonstone in Game of Thrones.
