Casco Viejo in Bilbao is the historic district of the city, known for its narrow, winding streets, colorful buildings, and bars and restaurants serving pintxos. It is the heart of old Bilbao, full of landmarks such as the Basilica of Saint James and traditional shops. The Plaza Nueva, a central square, often hosts local events and markets.

Guggenheim Museum is a world-renowned modern and contemporary art museum located in Bilbao, Spain. Designed by architect Frank Gehry, its striking, futuristic building is considered an architectural masterpiece.

A trip from Bilbao to Gaztelugatxe is a popular day excursion. Gaztelugatxe is a small rocky island on the Basque coast, famous for its dramatic scenery and winding stone staircase with over 200 steps leading to a hermitage at the top.

San Mamés Stadium is the home of Athletic Club Bilbao and one of the most iconic football stadiums in Spain. Known as "The Cathedral of Football," it opened in 2013 and hosts exciting matches as well as tours for visitors.
