Sopelana is known for its impressive cliffs and great waves, making it a favorite spot for surfers. It’s just a short metro ride from Bilbao and offers breathtaking coastal views and walking trails.

Barrika Barrika is known for its dramatic rock formations and scenic beauty. The cliffs and unique geological structures make it a favorite among photographers and hikers. It's also a quiet place to relax.

Gorrondatxe beach is a more rugged, natural beach with dark sand and grassy dunes. It’s less crowded and perfect for those looking to enjoy nature and tranquility. Popular with surfers and nature lovers alike.

Arrigunaga beach is located in Getxo, near Bilbao. It's a quiet beach with golden sand, backed by green cliffs and great views of the sea. It's perfect for relaxing, walking, or enjoying a peaceful sunset. The beach is also close to the old Algorta neighborhood and the famous cliffs of La Galea.
